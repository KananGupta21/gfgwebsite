Geeks For Geeks Student Chapter RSCOE

The vision of GeeksforGeeks Student Chapter is to foster a vibrant coding culture among students by promoting peer-to-peer learning and practical skill development. It aims to provide networking opportunities and internship pathways, enabling members to connect with industry professionals. Ultimately, the chapters strive to prepare students for successful careers in technology through collaboration and innovation.


GeeksforGeeks Student Chapter is university-based communities designed to cultivate a coding culture among students interested in Computer Science and technology. By being a part of it, students can:

  • Internship Opportunities: Gain valuable experience through internships at GeeksforGeeks.
  • Peer Learning: Enhance their knowledge in a collaborative environment with fellow students.
  • Real-World Projects: Work on solutions for local businesses, applying the latest technology trends.
  • Networking: Build connections with like-minded peers across various colleges.

Highlights of the club

GRE MOCK EXAM 2024

Name: The GRE Mock Exam was organized to guide students in preparing for the Graduate Record Examination. It started with an expert-led session offering strategies for verbal reasoning, quantitative analysis, and analytical writing. This was followed by a full length mock exam, giving students a chance to evaluate their performance and identify areas for improvement. The event provided valuable insights and boosted participants' confidence, making it a key step in their academic journey abroad.

F.Y INDUCTION 2024

The GeeksforGeeks Induction Program was conducted to welcome and guide first-year students, introducing them to the club's mission and activities. The session highlighted learning opportunities in coding, problem solving, and tech development, encouraging students to explore their potential. Through interactive discussions, students were made aware of upcoming workshops, competitions, and resources. The induction aimed to inspire participation and build a strong foundation in the tech community on campus.

SESSION:BEYOND ACADEMICS.

A session on "Beyond Academics" by Mukund Raut, Co-Founder of StudX, inspired students to explore their engineering journey beyond textbooks. With over 15 years of experience in Ed Tech, Mr. Raut shared real-world skills, industry insights, and strategies for personal and professional growth. The session motivated students to embrace innovation and align their academic pursuits with practical applications, leaving them with valuable guidance for a successful career.
